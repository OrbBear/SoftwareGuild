The Dog Gone Story
One day, our founder, Don Doggard visited downtown Dallas during a disastrous downpour. Despite the debilitating drenching, Doggard doggedly ducked downward into the dimly lit purveyor of diesel to dry himself. The dimly lit dwelling contained within a damn good dog in a diminutive department away from the downpour. From the first bite, Doggard knew Denton needed its own version and Dog Gone Good Hotdogs was born. Pictured above is our grill on which all the great items from our menu are cooked.
